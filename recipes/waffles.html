Waffles Recipe

Description

If you have leftover eggnog from the holidays you can use the rest of it to create a delicous breakfast! This recipe makes 4 servings of waffles so you can share this delicous treat with a friend or loved one. Make sure you have your favorite waffle toppings ready to add for added flavors

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups eggnog
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl. Add eggnog, eggs, and butter; mix well.
  2. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions. Pour 1/4 of the batter into the preheated iron and cook until waffle is crisp and golden, about 5 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
