Ramen Recipe

Description

This tasty ramen recipe is perfect for a cold day and you want a comfort food that will leave you feeling full and satisfied. In just 20 minutes, 10 prepping and 10 cooking, you can have delicous ramen!

This Ramen recipe is quick and easy to serve yourself and if you want to serve multiple people just double each ingredient. Feel free to add some of your own favorite ingredients such as an egg or extra seasonings to add more flare to this already amazing dish!

ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup cubed cooked chicken
  • 1/8 teaspoon dried minced onion, or to taste
  • 4 pinches Creole seasoning
  • 1 dash hot sauce
  • 1(3 ounce) package ramen noodle with seasoing packet
  • 1 slice pepper Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Pour water into a medium saucepan. Add chicken, onion, Creole seasoning, and hot sauce. Bring mixture to a rolling boil. Add noodles and reduce heat to medium; cook for 3 minutes.
  2. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the included noodle seasoning packet and pepper Jack cheese. Let cool slightly before serving.
